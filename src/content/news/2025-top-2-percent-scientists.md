We are proud to announce that Prof. Sang-Soo Baek, the principal investigator of EMAIL Lab, has been named in the **Elsevier & Stanford University World's Top 2% Scientists 2025** list.

This recognition highlights the lab's contributions to environmental modeling, artificial intelligence, and machine learning for water quality and ecological engineering.

Congratulations to Prof. Baek and to all lab members whose collaborative research made this achievement possible.
