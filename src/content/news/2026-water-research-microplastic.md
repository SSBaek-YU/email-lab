Our study, **"Multimodal artificial intelligence (AI) for estimating the sinking velocity of microplastic–microalgae aggregates using explainable AI and visualization,"** has been published in *Water Research*.

Led by Min Jeong Cho with co-authors Minhyuk Jeung, C. H. Lee, Y. K. Lim, K. D. Sudusinghe, S. H. Baek, and corresponding author Prof. Sang-Soo Baek, the work combines multimodal deep learning with explainable AI to understand the transport behavior of microplastics in aquatic environments.

Congratulations to the whole team!
