Our paper, **"Energy-efficient water quality modeling using memristor-based neuromorphic computing,"** has been published in *npj Clean Water*.

Authored by Minhyuk Jeung, G. Son, M. Kim, and corresponding author Prof. Sang-Soo Baek, the study explores how memristor-based neuromorphic hardware can dramatically reduce the energy cost of environmental modeling — a step toward sustainable AI for water research.

Congratulations to the authors!
